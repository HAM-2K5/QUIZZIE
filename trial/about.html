About QUIZZIE

Welcome to QUIZZIE, the ultimate quiz app designed to challenge your mind, sharpen your knowledge, and keep you entertained! Whether you're a trivia enthusiast, a student preparing for exams, or just someone looking for a fun way to learn new things — QUIZZIE has something for everyone.

Key Features:

  • Wide Range of Categories: PHY CHE MATHS and more.
  • Randomized Questions: Never get bored — every quiz is a new adventure!
  • Timed Quizzes: Race against time and test how quickly you can think on your feet.
  • Skip & Negative Marking: Smart scoring system to reward accuracy and strategy.

With QUIZZIE, learning feels like a game — because it is! Ready to test your brainpower and have some fun? Let's play!
